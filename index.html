Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the platform.

What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards and debit cards through Stripe. All payments are processed securely and we never store your card details.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes, you can cancel your Agency subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
Is my data secure? +
We use industry-standard encryption and security measures. Read our Privacy Policy for more information.
How do I get started as a Talent? +
Create an account, complete your profile with your portfolio and social links, and start getting discovered. Our team reviews profiles within 24-48 hours.
Are there any hidden fees? +
No hidden fees. Agencies pay their monthly subscription, and talents use the platform completely free. All pricing is transparent.
Can I delete my account and data? +
Yes, you have full control. You can delete your account anytime and we permanently remove all your information within 30 days.
